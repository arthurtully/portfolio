Advanced Streaming Setup

Daniel or better known as Flash has an amazing dual streaming setup that needed a lot of fine-tuning. We collaborated for a few days to get things running as smoothly as possible.

But we ended up having to redo everything to better suit his demands. One of the most serious problems was with the Elgato Camlink that was paired with Sony A6100. While debugging, I ended up updating his motherboard's BIOS because it was the actual source of the problem.

He was happy that he had finally solved the problem and told me how he had recruited a couple of other individuals to help him with it but none of them could. With everything out of the way, we could go to work on the Elgato Stream Deck, PlayStation capture card, and his streaming PC. We also ended up implementing a simple method for him to switch keyboards from one PC to another so he wouldn't have to use two sets. As a way to better optimize his brand and also have a streamlined user expirience for his merch store we setup a custom domain. It's currently pointing at his Streamlabs tipping.

I ended up working on the FEARLESS montage for him after the initial setup, however he has now moved on to a different project and has left streaming for now.
